PROGRAM OF WORK FOR 24-nd SEASON 2012-2013

( Musical drawing room of the Nikolaev regional art museum by V.V. Vereschagin, 4-st Sundays of month, 14 hours)

    1. Open a 24 seasons - general meeting of club: the report, the statement of working bodies and programm works of club for 2012-2013. The evening, devoted to the 75 anniversary of the Nikolaev region and the 223 anniversary of Nikolaev, - on September, 23rd, 2012, leader E.Farberova, responsible A.Zolotuhin.

    2. The thematically-musical composition, devoted to 200-years anniversary of the composer « the Europe spoilt child Orfey» Rossini, - on October, 28th, 2012, responsible S.Margulis.

    3. Creative evening of the poetess and translator Dariny Berezina with participation of Student's Theatre of University of Culture "STUK", - on November, 25th, 2012, responsible N. Nagornjak.

    4. The creative evening, devoted to the 50 anniversary of National drama theatre of the Honorary member of club, the Honored artist of Georgia, Nikolay Troyanov, - on December, 23rd, 2012, responsible A.Zolotuhin, L.Odegova.

    5. Anniversary creative evening of the pianist and the teacher of the Higher musical school of Nataliya Papushenko, on January, 27th, 2013, responsible L.I.Odegova.

    6. Evening of personal creativity of members of Pushkin club, 24 February 2013, leader N. Nagornjak.

    7. The world day of poetry with participation of poets Nikolaev region, devoted to the 100 anniversary from of Mark Lisjanskogo's birth, - on March, 24th, 2013, leader N.S.Nagornjak and E.M.Farberova.

    8. Literary-musicale «Culture of an epoch of education: France and Ukraine», - on April, 28th, 2013, - leaders T.S.Kairova and O.N.Petrenko.

    9. Creative evening of a member of the Union of Artists of Ukraine, Volodimer Fedorchenko, - on May, 26th, 2013, responsibles A.Zolotuhin, L.Odegova's.

    10. Birthday of A.S. Pushkin, Thursday, June 6, 2013 - 214 years!
    - - 11 hours. – laying of colours for memorial boards of Pushkin;
    - - 12 hours – for a monument Pushkin;
    Closing of a season.
