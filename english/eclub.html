NIKOLAEV PUSHKIN Club

ABOUT Club

The Nikolaev Pushkin Club - the nongovernmental homing public organization created on October, 19th, 1989 and acting on territories of city of Nikolaev at Nikolaev a regional art museum to name after somebody of V.V. Vereschagin. The purpose of work of Club is preservation, development and propagation of Russian culture on the best samples of world culture, a creative heritage of genius A.S. Pushkin (look most the important Pushkin sites) and a cultural study of local lore heritage.

Into tasks of Club enters: preservation, studying and propagation of the Pushkin heritage, the memorable places connected with it history of persons from his environment, connections with Ukraine; increase of culture of perception of a Pushkin heritage, assistance humanities systems of education and national education, education of love to native territory and city; assistance to revival of all national cultures.

For realization of objects in view and tasks Club: conducts researches of a life and A.S. Pushkin's creativity and his connection with Nikolaev region; conduct literary-musicale soiree, including with other national societies; promotes development of museum work on the basis of Pushkin a heritage and Pushkin study of local lore; promotes the publication of works of the members; collaborate with schools, institutes, libraries, museums with the purpose of improvement of teaching and propaganda the Russian classical literature; acts with initiatives before local and a main line authorities concerning preservation of memorable places and monuments. In 2006 are made the list of Honorary members of the Club which has influenced for work of Club, and 10 commandment of Club.

The Club builds the work on principles of voluntariness, support of the common and personal initiatives, operates as independent body of the public. Over work of Club the council elected annually on general meeting of Club on which the program of work of Club for a forthcoming season affirms also supervises. The season, by tradition, opens general meeting of the Club, coming to the end a concert in September, and is closed by celebrating of birthday of the poet on June, 6th. The Club during a season monthly, in 4-th Sunday of each month conduct literary-musicale soiree. From the date of the basis the Club works in the Musical drawing room of V.V. Vereschagin art museum. Owing to long cooperation of Pushkin Club with the Art museum in it is collected the Pushkin fund numbering more of 2000 units of storage.
