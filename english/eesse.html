In this connection I offer to your attention a Christmas essay
"And nowadays, and always, and in ages of centuries below... ", devoted 200-years from birthday of the Poet.
But, before some words about itself. I had a happy about 19 of years of the life to devote to study of life and creativity A.S.Pushkin, which was in Nikolaev, and of V.I.Dahl, childhood and which youth have passed in Nikolaev. During researches I have visited more than 40 archives and libraries in Moscow, St.-Petersburg, Odessa, Nikolaev, Kherson, Kirovograd, Sevastopol, Kharkov, Kiev, worked and in the Pushkin House (StP.), where all manuscripts of the Poet are assembled. In result I solve secret of destruction Pushkin, it was possible to specify details of navigation of the Poet on warship "Mingreliya", on which he has written one of most remarkable elegy "A day time star went out... ". And restore model warship to solve secret of trip Pushkin " on a locust". The assumption is stated and the proofs to are given that in the beginning of October, 1823 Pushkin secretly came to Nikolaev from Odessa on a yacht "Utekha", that has become the original reason of dispatch him from Odessa in Mikhaylovskoe. It was possible to find three houses, in which V.I.Dahl lived in Nikolaev to prove, that Dahl did not write calumnious poems on the spouse of the admiral Greyg. In Nikolaev Pushkin, on my researches, has written a beginning of the letter Tatiana to Onegin and some stanzas from 1-st and 3-rd chapters of "Evgeniy Onegin". On the basis of researches the historical information's are prepared and are established in Nikolaev a memorial board (1987) and monument to the Poet (1988). By me is written and more than 30 clauses about Pushkin and Local Lore are issued in local press, beginning from ancient Greek of times. October 19, 1999 will execute 10 years to Pushkin club, which I have created also with which lead. On this page Internet are resulted: The items of information about Pushkin club, structure of its honorable members and Program of work on an anniversary season, some of most important my clauses about Pushkin and Dahl. Using a case, from a name of Pushkin club, I express gratitude provider the CityNetWork for the given resources Internet..
