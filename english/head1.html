THE PROGRAM OF WORK OF THE PUSHKIN CLUB ON A SEASON 1998-1999 YEARS.
(Musical drawing-room of an Art museum by V.V.Verschagin, Sunday, 14.00)


1. General meeting of club: the report, statement of working bodies and programs of work of club on a season 1998-1999 years. Opening of a season, concert "Poetry of Pushkin", readers of club with participation of the winners of competitions the singer Galina Ivannikova and pianist Andrey Vasin, September 27, 1998.
2. Literary - musical evening "Pushkin and culture of France", together with "Alliance Frances", - October 25, 1998. Responsible: Kairova T.S.
3. Literary - musical evening "Pushkin, Dahl and Nikolaev " - November 22, 1998, Responsible: Zolotukhin A.I.
4. Literary musical evening "Pushkin in creativity of the Nikolaev artists " - December 27, 1998. Responsible: Shvartsman E.I.
5. Literary musical evening "Pushkin and Ukraine", with an exhibition of the books of Regional scientific library - January 24, 1999. Responsible: Zolotukhin A.I., Ivanova I.F.
6. Realization of open scientific hearings devoted 200-years from birthday A.S.Pushkin, together with Pedagogical institute, audience of Pedagogical institute, February 19, 1999, from 14 hours. Responsible: Zolotukhin A.I., Miroshnichenko E.G.
7. Literary - musical evening: A.S.Pushkin - "Mozart and Sal'ery", in execution of National theatre of A.N.Troyanova - February 28, 1999. Responsible: Zolotukhin A.I.
8. Literary musical evening " Pushkin about the women and women about Pushkin " - March 28, 1999, Woman of club responsible for organization Shvartsman E.I.
9. Literary - musical evening "The poets - writers the contemporaries of Pushkin" - April 25, 1999 . Responsible for organization: Farberova E.M., Semionova S.A.
10. Easter meetings, evening of memory of the artist IU.T.Steshina-May 23,1999. -Responsible: Roslyakov S.N.
11. Preparation and edition "Of Works Nikolaev of Pushkin club" under the newspaper publications The members of club: preparation of a breadboard model - December, 1998; search of sources of financing - January 1999; the edition - May, 1999. Editorial board: Zolotukhin A.I., A.P.Dai'yanenko, V.P.Chystov, I.F.Ivanova, A.G.Iiroshnichenko.
12. Day of birth Pushkin, June 6, 1999 - 200 years! The basic measures:
- The all-city evening - performance "Pushkin and Nikolaev ", Russian theatre - June 3, at 16 hours.;
- At a memorial board Pushkin (corner str.str. Shevchenko and Soviet) members of Pushkin club is assign flowers, reading of poems - the 6-th of June (Sunday) at 11.00 hour.;
- At a monument Pushkin, reading of poems - the 6-th of June (Sunday) at 12.00 hour.
Responsible: for the script of performance - Zolotukhin A.I., for produce - Dem'yanenko A.P., decorating of a stage - Roslyakov S.N.
13. Preparation and edition of the booklet "Pushkin and Nikolaev", devoted 200-years of the poet, May, 1999 . Responsible Miryan A.N., Zolotukhin A.I.
14. Closing a season. Literary - musical evening devoted 110-years Anna Akhmatova "A cleanest of sound is high authority", June 27, 1999. Responsible: Farberova E.M.
