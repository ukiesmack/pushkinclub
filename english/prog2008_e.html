    2. Literary-musical evening dedicated reading of the new play of Nikolay Troyanov " Dreams Roll of Panna", - October 28, 2007, which is responsible S. Roslyakov, O. Petrenko.

    3. Literary - musical evening dedicated presentation of the new books: T. Peresun'ko " Zoschen-ko and Nikolaev ", T. Moskalenko " Silver a thread " and novel by Mezina " Order of heavens ", - November 25, 2007, which is responsible T. Moskalenko.

    4. Evenings Sacred of Sesill: " By a Wind the smoke from a candle on a drawing room delivers", - December 23, 2007, which is responsible T. Kairova.

    5. Creative evening of the teacher of a Musical school of Papushenko, January 27, 2008, which is responsible - N. Papushenko, L. Odegova.

    6. Evening of personal creativity of the members of club dedicated to A. Pushkin, - February 24, 2008, which is responsible E. Farberova, T. Moskalenko.

    7. World wide de day of poetry, including of Soyuz of the writers of the Nikolaev region, - March 23, 2008, which is responsible Т. Moskalenko, V. Kachurin.

    8. Literary - musical evening dedicated the founder of our culture to Ilarion-Nikon-Boyan (997-1088), April 27, 2008, - responsible A. Zolotukhin, O. Petrenko.

    9. Creative evening of the poet of Vyacheslav Kachurin in Pushkin club, May 25 2008г., responsible, A. Zolotukhin.

    10. Birthday of Pushkin, Friday, June 6, 2008 - 209 years!
