PROGRAM OF WORK FOR 23-nd SEASON 2011-2012

( Musical drawing room of the Nikolaev regional art museum by V.V. Vereschagin, 4-st Sundays of month, 14 hours)

    1. Open a 23 seasons - general meeting of club: the report, the statement of working bodies and Programme works of club for 2011-2012. Creative evening of the poet, the winner of the State premium by T.G. Shevchenko, of Dmytro Kremin', - on September, 25th, 2011, leading Taras Kremin, A.Zolotuhin and N.Nagornjak.

    2. The musicale, devoted to 50-years anniversary of composer Eliny Obraztcova, - on October, 23rd, 2011, responsible N.Nagornjak.

    3. Literary evening of a member of the union of writers of Ukraine of Anatoly Maljarov, - on November, 27th, 2011, responsible A. Zolotukhin.

    4. The evening, devoted to 75 anniversary of Mykola Vingranovs'kiy, - 18 December, leader T.Kremin.

    5. The creative evening, devoted to the 90 anniversary of the Honorary member of club, the Honored artist of Georgia, Nikolay Troyanov, - on December, 25th, 2011, responsibles A.Zolotuhin, L.Odegova.

    6. Evening of personal creativity of members of Pushkin club, - on January, 22nd, 2012 leader N.Nagornjak.

    7. Creative evening of the leader of the Nikolaev state college of culture of Natalia Krumilenko, - on February, 26th, 2012, leader Larissa Bovduj.

    8. The world day of poetry with participation of poets Nikolaev region - on March, 25th, 2012, leader N.S.Nagornjak.

    9. The literary and art evening, devoted to the 200 anniversary of Patriotic War 1812, - on April, 22nd, 2012, - leaders E.M.Farberova and L.I.Odegova.

    10. Creative evening of a member of the Union of Artists of Ukraine, the winner of the State premium by T.G. Shevchenko, Michael Ozerny, - on May, 27th, 2012, responsibles A.Zolotuhin, L.Odegova's.

    11. Birthday of A.S. Pushkin, Wednesday, June 6, 2012 - 213 years!
