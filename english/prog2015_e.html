PROGRAM OF WORK FOR 26-nd SEASON 2014-2015

( Musical drawing room of the Nikolaev regional art museum by V.V. Vereschagin, 4-st Sundays of month, 14 hours)

1. Opening - general meeting of club: approval of the program of work of the club in the 26th season. Report of Zolotukhin "Where Odysseus sailed? Evidence!" - 28 September 2014, leading Zolotukhin.

2. Participation in the events dedicated to the 100th anniversary of the "Art Museum of V.V. Vereshchagin", - Tuesday, 28 October 2014, responsible S.N. Roslyakov.

3. Literary-musical evening dedicated to the 170th anniversary of N.A. Rimskiy-Korsakov, - 23 November, 2014, a leading S. Margulis.

4. Troyanovskays reading, involving Troyanova Theatre, director. S. Chverkalyuk, - December 28, 2014, responsible N. Nagornyak.

5. Evening personal creativity members dedicated to A.S. Pushkin, - 25 January 2015, responsible N. Nagornyak.

6. Benefit Student Theatre "STUK", the head of Artem Svistun, - February 22, 2015, responsible N. Nagornyak

7. Performance of the musical ensemble "Orpheus", head. V. Gorbenko and bard Viktor Panchenko, - March 22, 2015, responsible N. Nagornyak.

8. Literary Evening "Ukraine and the European world in the Age of Enlightenment", leading Kairova T. and A. Petrenko, - April 26, 2015

9. Evening devoted to the work traveler, journalist and artist Yuri Korolev, - 24 May 2015, leading S.N. Roslyakov and A. Zolotukhin.

10. Birthday of A.S. Pushkin, Saturday, June 6, 2015 - 216 years!
- - 11 hours. – laying of colours for memorial boards of Pushkin;
- - 12 hours – for a monument Pushkin;

11. The Final Season. Accounting concert composer of Elina Obraztsova, - 14 h., Saturday, June 20, 2016
